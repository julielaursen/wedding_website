Our wedding is at Star Hill Ranch and we would like you to be a part of it. Centered along a historic main street and surrounded by panoramic Hill Country views, Star Hill is located just 25 minutes west of downtown Austin in the community of Bee Cave. Star Hill is currently home to 22 historic buildings that have been relocated from around Texas. All were originally constructed between 1850 and 1935.

How do I get there?

From the airport/downtown, take Hwy 71 West to Bee Caves, TX and turn left on Hamilton Pool Rd

What's the dress code?!

Ceremony 4PM - 5PM

Sunday Best
We ask that you wear something nice that looks good in photos! We'd prefer no logo shirts, crop tops, cargo shorts or running shoes. Although our venue is both indoor/outdoor, Texas can be hot and humid well into the fall. Therefore, we don't recommend wool or heavy clothing. Polo short sleeve shirts, sundresses, jumpsuits and cocktail dresses all work. The venue includes many historic buildings from the 1800s and early 1900s. Vintage clothing, flapper dresses, and western wear would all fit the venue well.

Reception 7PM - 12AM

Dance Wear Encouraged
We'll be dancing the night away so we highly recommend either dancing shoes or cowboy boots. Ladies, feel free to bring an extra pair of shoes if you want to change from sandals/heels to dance shoes. We will have room to change if needed.

What's your COVID-19 policy?
We require our guests to be vaccinated or have a doctor's note if unable to get vaccinated for medical reasons
Are children allowed?
>We respectfully wish to keep this an adult only wedding
Is the venue BYOB?
No! TABC has strict rules about this. Only bartenders can serve alcohol at this venue. As a bonus, the bartenders love to talk about the history of the venue.
OPEN BAR...?
OPEN BAR! We will have beer, wine and champagne. Check out the old saloon (circla 1873) which was originally the Bee Cave Post Office & Store or the biergarten (1850s) relocated from Industry, Texas.
What events are scheduled besides the ceremony and reception?
We will have a rehearsal dinner that is invite only. No other events are currently scheduled.
Are gifts required?
We are happy to accept all gifts including a thoughtful card or your favorite cooking recipe (no garlic).
Is there anything important for me to know as a guest?
Let us know about changes! If you RSVP but later need to cancel, please inform the bride and groom as early as possible. Catering and seating is per head and advanced notice allows us to recoup those costs.
Is there anything important to know about the venue?
This is very important! Our venue includes many wood buildings that are over 100 years old. Please refrain from any smoking anywhere near the venue itself. There will be a designated smoking area a short walking distance from the buildings. Also, please refrain from going inside any buildings that are not designated with a sign in front of them. Buildings not directly involved in the ceremony/reception are considered private. Also, all decorations and centerpieces are rentals from the venue and must be left at the end of the night as they are not favors but rentals.
How is parking?
should be ample room for parking at the venue.
What will the catering consist of?
Please let us know if you have special dietary needs we need to accomodate. Our catering is provided by Verde's mexican restaurant and our entrees are going to be chicken or pork with rice or sweet potatoes, grilled vegetables, and appetizers. We will also have grilled cheese and grilled peanut butter sandwiches for late night and a barista truck in the late afternoon/early evening.
What activities are on the schedule for the wedding?
We hope to be one of the most fun weddings you've attended. Between cocktail hour and the afterparty, part of the schoolhouse building will have tables for board games. Main street will have cornhole and giant jenga. Ralph Fisher's photo animal company will be providing beer burros and armadillo racing. Armadillo racing will come with prizes! We will also have an axe-throwing booth! Late Night: Post send-off we will hav a dancing after-hours party with a DJ and catering by Burro's artisan grilled cheese. Expect a mix of East Coast Swing, West Coast Swing, Two Step and Lindy.
Social Media/Photography
The venue itself is a great photo opportunity. Though we ask that guests do not photograph or video the wedding (we have professionals covering that), take as many pictures of yourself as you'd like in front of the historic buildings and vehicles.

What's there to do in Bee Caves, TX?
Our venue is a 2 minute drive from the Hill Country Galleria, which includes multiple restaurants and breweries as well as lots of shopping, Whole Foods, and a Gold's gym. On Saturday, the Hill Country Galleria may throw concerts or have a farmers market. See their website for details. Our venue is also very close to Dripping Springs, Hamilton Pool and lots of breweries and wineries. The venue is also a short drive from Lake Travis in Lakeway and the Ladybird Johnson Ladybird Johnson Wildflower Center in South Austin. We'd recommend The Oasis in Lakeway for music and margaritas with a view.
